Cuando al fin

Atrapé a aquel ladrón:

Mi propio hijo

Poesía estilo Senryu sobre el go, por William Pinckard

https://www.kiseidopublishing.com/sen.htm

Traducciones al español: Ángel Sesma

Tatta hito-ban to

uchihajimeta wa

sakujitsu nari

Saying `just one game'

they began to play . . .

That was yesterday.

Comienzan

Dicen “un juego”

Hace ayer

Gokaisho to

isha to e tsukai

futari dashi

To the go club and

to the doctor's, a pair

of servants is despatched.

Al doitor

Y al club de go

Dos mozos

Shincho ni

utsu wa saisho no

ichi ni ban

With caution and prudence

they play the first

one or two games.

Primer juego

Cautela, prudencia

¿O segundo?

Go no jogen

iitakunaru to

niwa e tachi

Go advice:

when he wants to speak out

he steps into the garden.

Mi consejo:

¿Lo sabes ya bien?

¡Anda afuera!

Go no rusu e

maotoko utte --

gae ni kuru

He's out playing go.

Her lover, for a snap --

back, comes in.

Él ya juega.

Ella y un amante:

"A snap-back"

Sumi ni ishi

korosarete kara

O-ki ga tsuku

The stones in the corner --

after they have been killed

he starts to worry . . .

Ya muertas

Piedras de esquina

Ya se preocupa

Ishi wa iki-

ta ga shini-me ni wa

awanu nari

His stones have

lived, but for the deathbed

he is not in time.

Grupo que vive

Último adiós

Ese ya no fue

Sensei ni

yon moku, deshi ni mo

yon moku nari

With the teacher,

it's four stones; with the pupil too,

it's four stones.

Al sensei,

Cuatro piedras, cuatro

Al pupilo

Ki ni makete

kaereba uchi wa

arashi nari

Losing at go,

when he returns the house

becomes a tempest.

Perdió al shudan

Al regresar a casa

Un aguacero

O-aite no

go ni kachiso de

kangaeru

With an exalted person

playing go, and in danger of

winning, he thinks hard.

Exaltado en el go

En peligro de ganar

Piensa y piensa

Go no shobu

yobu koe de shiru

nyobo kana

Outcome of the game:

by the voice that calls her,

the wife knows.

El resultado

Por como él la llama

Ella lo sabe

Kaku utaba

nado to hebo go no

make-oshimi

`If only I had played here'

etc.,etc. -- the go klutz's

pain at losing.

¿y, tal jugada?

Dolores de los torpes

Que juegan al go.

Shoben ni

okite nyobo wa

go wo shikari

Getting up

to piss, the housewife

scolds the go game.

Levantándose

Ella a mear, habla:

¡Maldito juego!

Yoyaku ni

katte go kyaku ga

toki wo kiki

At long last

winning, the go guest

asks the time.

Al fin y al cabo

Ganó el invitado

Pide la hora

Yo fukasi ni

tenjo made ga

go-ban nari

In the depth of night

even the ceiling

becomes a go board.

El cielo raso

En la noche profunda

Tablero de go

Go narabaya

ko ni mo tatete

iku beki wo

shinuru michi ni wa

te hitotsu mo nashi

If this were go

I'd start a ko fight

and surely live,

but on the road to death

there's no move left at all.

Si jugara al go

Pelearía por el ko

Y viviría

Camino a la muerte

No hay más juego.
